# Sample Customer Requests

## Request 1: Billing dispute
"Hi, I just noticed a charge of $249.99 on my credit card from your company. I cancelled my subscription last month and should not be charged. Please refund this immediately."

## Request 2: Technical issue
"Your mobile app keeps crashing when I try to upload photos. I've tried reinstalling but it still happens. This is really frustrating because I need to send photos to my clients."

## Request 3: Feature request
"It would be great if you could add a dark mode to the dashboard. I work late at night and the bright screen is hard on my eyes. Many of my colleagues have mentioned this too."

## Request 4: General inquiry
"Hello, I'm interested in your enterprise plan. Could you please send me information about pricing and what features are included? We have about 50 users."

## Request 5: Complaint
"I've been waiting 3 days for a response to my support ticket #12345. This is unacceptable. Your website says you respond within 24 hours. I need this resolved today or I'm considering cancelling."
