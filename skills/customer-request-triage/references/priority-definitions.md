# Priority Definitions

## Critical

- Service completely unavailable for the customer
- Data loss or security breach suspected
- Customer explicitly threatens legal action
- VIP or enterprise account with high revenue impact

## High

- Core functionality is broken but a workaround exists
- Customer is unable to complete a purchase or key transaction
- Multiple customers reporting the same issue
- Issue has been open for more than 48 hours without resolution

## Medium

- Non-core functionality is impaired
- Customer is frustrated but can still use the service
- Single customer reporting an issue
- Feature request from a paying customer

## Low

- General question or inquiry
- Nice-to-have feature suggestion
- Cosmetic issues or minor inconveniences
- Non-urgent account management requests
