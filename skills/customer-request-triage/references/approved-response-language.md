# Approved Response Language

## Approved phrases

- "Thank you for contacting us. I've reviewed your request and here's what I found:"
- "I understand your concern. Let me look into this for you."
- "Your request has been received and is being reviewed by our team."
- "I've routed your request to the appropriate team. You can expect a response within [timeframe]."
- "I want to make sure I understand correctly. Could you clarify [specific point]?"
- "I appreciate your patience while we work on this."

## Prohibited phrases

- "I promise this will be fixed by [specific time]." (Never commit to resolution times without team confirmation)
- "This is not our problem." (Always take ownership)
- "You should have [done X]." (Never blame the customer)
- "I don't know and I can't help." (Always offer escalation)
- "That's against our policy." (Reframe positively: "Here's what I can do instead:")
- "Calm down." (Never dismiss customer emotion)

## Tone guidelines

- Be empathetic without being patronizing
- Be direct without being curt
- Be specific without being overwhelming
- Take ownership without overcommitting
